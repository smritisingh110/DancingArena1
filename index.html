ABOUT US

Rhythmic Moves Arena is home to two vibrant entities - The Rhythmic Moves Academy and The Rhythmic Moves Performance Troupe.

The Rhythmic Moves Academy, founded by renowned choreographer Emma Rodriguez, began its journey in the world of dance in January 2000. Inspired by international dance institutions, the academy offers a diverse curriculum that covers a spectrum of styles including Hip-Hop, Latin Dance, Contemporary, Ballroom, and Street Jazz.

Our academy has successfully nurtured a diverse community of students, with our reach expanding across the city and its outskirts, creating a dance family that is truly interconnected.

The Rhythmic Moves Performance Troupe, curated from our talented senior students, operates under the guidance of Artistic Director Emma Rodriguez. These emerging artists are not only performers but also educators and choreographers. The troupe follows a unique curriculum crafted by our Artistic Director, fostering their growth and creativity.

THE PHILOSOPHY

At Dance Harmony, our guiding philosophy is rooted in the belief that dance is a transformative journey, a pathway to self-discovery and personal growth.

We aspire to empower individuals to uncover and develop their unique dance style, fostering an environment of joy, camaraderie, and shared values. Our philosophy revolves around the idea of helping people tap into their full potential, both as dancers and as individuals.

We instill in our students the principles of self-belief, discipline, and goal-setting, guiding them to apply these skills not only within the dance realm but also in their chosen vocations, whatever they may be. Our commitment is to create a space where the love for dance harmonizes with personal development, creating well-rounded individuals ready to face the world.

THE VISION

Our vision at Dance Elevation is to foster a heightened awareness and understanding of international dance, cultivating an environment that nurtures and supports the continuous evolution of artists.

We believe in the facilitation of artists on their journey of growth. Beyond teaching dance steps, our approach encompasses a broader understanding of self, creativity, movement, and physiology. We empower our students with knowledge, encouraging them to go beyond imitation and embrace the path of self-discovery.

Our goal is to equip dancers not only with technical skills but also with the tools to create their own unique works of art. We envision a community where dancers can explore, innovate, and ultimately develop a distinctive movement style that reflects their individuality.
